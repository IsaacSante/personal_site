About

Hello I'm Isaac Sante, a designer and creative developer. My time is spent programming innovative real time graphics for the web and live performances. My aim with these projects is to create submersive storytelling experiences and redefine what it means to explore the web.

Im currently a research assisant for artist Ayo Okunseinde, developing a website with speculative 3D artifacts from an Afro-Futurist timeline. Im also finishing my degree at the Design & Technology program at Parsons School of Design, New York.

I also specialize in other areas of design, like film & graphic design for which I started my own practice, Black Swan Design. My company is currently working with various clients on writing/filming/directing for advertisements and marketing campaigns.
